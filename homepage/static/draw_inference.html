Welcome to the Artist Version

Navigate this website by drawing simple icons

🎯 How it works

Draw simple icons on the canvas to navigate between sections. The AI will recognize your drawing and take you to the corresponding page.

🚀 Technicalities

The model is a simple CNN powered by TFJS.

📚 Tutorial

Step 1: Understanding the Canvas

The drawing canvas is located on the right side of your screen. It's a 224x224 pixel area where you can draw simple icons using your mouse or finger.

Step 2: Drawing Recognition

The small AI model recognizes six different icons: home, envelope, person, suitcase, book, and X. Draw clearly and simply for best results.

Step 3: Navigation Magic

When the AI recognizes your drawing with over 90% confidence, it automatically navigates to the corresponding section.
